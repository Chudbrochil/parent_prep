# Packing for Parents

A free packing list web app for new parents. Installs to any phone's home screen, works offline, and keeps your lists on your own device.

**Live at [packingforparents.com](https://packingforparents.com)**

## What it is

A small Progressive Web App — a website that installs like a native app. Tap "Add to Home Screen" on your phone and it opens full-screen from your home icon, no app store required.

Four curated trip templates (short trip, short overnight, long trip, and an extended-stay list) plus a guided wizard that builds a personalized packing list from a few quick questions about your baby and your trip.

## Privacy

Everything lives on your phone. No accounts, no sign-ins, no cloud sync, no analytics, no tracking. Your packing lists never leave your device.
